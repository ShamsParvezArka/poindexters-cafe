My Nightmares

"Coming up with an original idea in 21st century is tough, even my dreams aren't original anymore!" ~ Poindexter

Last night, I saw something strange in my dream. I was in a mistical forest, sorrounded by thick fog. As I wandered through the mist,I saw a strange cat passing by. parts of it's fur were glowing. At first, she was running-away from me. But as time passed, her pace calm down and came close to me. The glow on its fur dimmed the close it came, until it was almost within my arm's reach. I gently placed my arm on its head ... ... ... dream faded

It reminded me of something-a bitter memory I often regret. I once had 2 bunnies. I raised them when they were only 20 days old. They grew up before my eyes. But They died because of me! I couldn't took proper care of them. I was the irresponsible one. This guild hunts me. Because of me, they never got to live their full lives.
